About Me

From the Classroom to the Console

I'm the founder of Foundry339 Web Lab, and before that, I spent years as a STEM educator — teaching students how to break big, complicated problems into small, understandable pieces. That's exactly the skill I bring to web design.

I build websites the same way I used to build lesson plans: start with the goal, strip out anything that gets in the way, and explain every decision in plain language instead of jargon. My background in graphic design and front-end development means I handle the look, the code, and the strategy behind it — so you're not stuck coordinating three different vendors.

My focus is simple: help small businesses build a stronger online presence and turn that presence into real traffic, real leads, and real customers.
